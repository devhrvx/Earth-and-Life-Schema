About Earth
Our home planet Earth is a rocky, terrestrial planet. It has a solid and active surface with mountains, valleys, canyons, plains and so much more. Earth is special because it is an ocean planet. Water covers 70% of Earth's surface.
  • Population: 8,116,698,258 (June 21, 2024)
  • Time on Earth: A day on Earth lasts a little under 24 hours.
    One year on Earth lasts 365.25 days. That 0.25 extra means every four years we need to add one day to our calendar. We call it a leap day (in a leap year).One year on Earth lasts 365.25 days.   • Moons: Earth has just one Moon. It is the only planet to have just one moon.
  • Systems: Earth has various systems, such as the Geosphere (land), Atmosphere (air), Biosphere (life), Hydrosphere (water), etc...
